Privacy Policy

OmniConverter respects your privacy. This privacy policy describes what data we collect and how it is used.

Data Collected

OmniConverter is a client-side web app. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

All data (such as recent conversions or favorites) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include analytics, ad tracking, or any third-party SDKs that send your data externally.

Permissions

This app does not request any special permissions. It only runs in the browser and operates fully offline once loaded.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the project repository.
